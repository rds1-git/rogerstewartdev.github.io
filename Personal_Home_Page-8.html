About

Hi my name is Roger ive lived in the West Midlands, specifically Birmingham, for most of my life. I studied at Sutton College, where I earned my HNC in Graphic Design over two years. I then pursued further studies at Dunstable College, achieving my HND in Graphic Design. After moving back to Walsall, I continued my education at Walsall College, completing my HNC and HND in Software Engineering over two years.

In 2006, I started my ringtone company, Bling Tones, which I ran for two years. In 2008, I launched a software company focused on educational software for children, covering Key Stages 1 and 2. This venture involved creating over four hundred lessons, designing a logo character, animating page layouts, and developing a fully functioning e-commerce website. I used tools like Photoshop and Dreamweaver, which enhanced my skills in Adobe Photoshop, Dreamweaver, Director CS, and coding with HTML and CSS. For this project, we built an app using Director and linked a database with SQL to allow users to log in and save their personal data, progress, and status.

My hobbies include watching anime and sci-fi movies, playing games, drawing, and game development. I am also committed to self-improvement and have enrolled in several online courses to enhance my development skills in C# and VB.NET, as well as Unity 3D. Over my spare time, I have developed more than 30 mini games and apps. My dream is to create software that people enjoy playing or that can improve their lives.
